About Me

Victoria Panos is an illustrator and designer born and raised in Toronto. She loves the tactile aspects of traditional art, and enjoys blending that with the versatility of digital media. In addition to graphic work, she also designs apparel and hats, and directs the country's hottest handbell ensemble.


She lives in the east end with two large cats and a small dog.
